About

My name is Margarita. I'm originally from Estonia - a small, but charming country in Northern Europe. Seeking a change from my previous role as an accountant , I decided to embark on an adventure to learn English and immerse myself in diverse cultures. This led me to the United States, where I became an au pair, caring for two wonderful children and gaining a whole new perspective on life.

During this time, I uncovered a deep passion for programming through my computer science courses at a community college. Motivated by this newfound love, I made the bold decision to further pursue my education. Transferring to the University of California San Diego, I recently completed my studies in Computer Science, seizing valuable opportunities to engage in student organizations and gain practical experience through internship, enriching my journey in this dynamic field.

When I'm not coding away, you'll find me out in the great outdoors. Hiking, biking, and walking are my go-to activities to connect with nature and find inner peace. Lately, I've also picked up tennis as a new hobby. Although I'm still a beginner, I can't help but dream of playing at Wimbledon someday. And if you happen to share my deep love for ramen, well, it's safe to say we're destined to get along!

As for my personality, I like to keep it real. I'm honest, hardworking, and down to earth. Being organized is my superpower. I could alphabetize your pantry and color-code your closet without breaking a sweat. So, if you're looking for someone who's not afraid to get their hands dirty in the world of technology, all while caring about the bigger picture, then I'm your girl.
